# Fortimark Cinematic Intro Video Folder

Place your cinematic intro video file in this directory and name it:
`chess-intro.mp4`

### Video Requirements:
1. **Filename:** Must be exactly `chess-intro.mp4` (all lowercase).
2. **Format:** MP4 (H.264 video codec and AAC audio codec are recommended for maximum web browser compatibility).
3. **Behavior:** The script will automatically play this video once, stop on the final frame (which should show your logo/black coin), and then fade in the headline "Marketing Isn't Broken. It's Just Disconnected." right below it!
